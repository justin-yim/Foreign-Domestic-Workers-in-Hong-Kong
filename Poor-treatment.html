Poor Treatment

Despite the tremendous support these foreign workers has given us, Hong Kong is lacking behind in protecting their rights.

A Hong Kong NGO, Justice Centre, has interviewed over 1,000 foreign domestic workers in 2016. 39% of respondents said they did not have their own bedroom, and over 35% shared a room with a child or elderly individual. Besides, 1 in 3 workers had been suffering from lack of food, and 58% constantly suffered from verbal abuse.
