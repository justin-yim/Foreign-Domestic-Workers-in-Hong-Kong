A Boost for the Female Workforce in Hong Kong
Hover over the line to see exact figures!

The import of foreign domestic workers has released a large portion of female the population into the labour force.

The labor force participation rate (LFPR) for female has grown from 47.5% in 1982 to 54.8% in 2016.

They are the backbone of Hong Kong's labour market and social care system.
